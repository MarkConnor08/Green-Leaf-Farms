Green Leaf Farms

Hello and Welcome to the Green Leaf farms!

In our Farm we have locally made produce, Exotic petting zoos and fun for the whole family play parks

We are located between Edinburgh and Carlisle, which is perfect for anyone looking for a fun stop for the day.

Kids come in for free!

The Petting zoo includes animals such as Horses and cows and even some more uncommon ones like meerkats and gerbils

You can also come into our cafe for some free samples of our produce.
