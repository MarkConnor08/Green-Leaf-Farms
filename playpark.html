Play Park Area

There are 2 different play park areas, One for children and one for teens.

The one for children is for ages 4 - 12. You must have a valid ticket to be able to enter.

The one for teens is for ages 13 - 18. You must have a valid ticket to be able to enter

Swings

Anyone can go on the swings!

There is no need to show your ticket as these swings are for everyone!
